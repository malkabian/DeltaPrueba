About Us

DeltaSport is your solution for quality, affordable, custom jerseys and apparel. Founded in 2024, we work closely with athletic teams to provide a simple and straightforward apparel ordering experience. Our custom order process maximizes the unique style of each team. Co-founded by Ultimate-Frisbee enthusiasts, Red and Katie, DeltaSport values a quality product at an affordable price. You share your vision, and we make it happen. We are proudly based in Maryland, USA.
